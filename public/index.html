Our Story

FROM KAMERON

My one word of advice to single men out there - learn to play the guitar. It gets all the ladies. In my case, it helped me land the woman I'm going to spend the rest of my life with. My guitar was with me the day I met Mackenzie and on the day I asked her to be wife (proposal video).

At the start, the universe seemed against us. Date #1: A dinner date in Old Town Fort Collins. My car gets towed and I show a great amount of restraint paying $325 and not losing my cool in front of Mackenzie. Date #2: A rockies game that gets rained out in the 7th inning. Date #3: A Dave Matthews Band concert. Mackenzie passes out and is taken to the EMT tent (I was able to recite her address and birthday at this point in time...creepy or romantic?).

But it's moments like these that remind me why I love this girl so much. They remind me that no matter what the world throws at us, she'll be by my side. Not only is she smart and beautiful, she's genuine. She opens my eyes to new perspectives. She is spontaneous yet easy going. She's an introvert like myself and enjoys sitting and watching a movie together. And she gives me free haircuts!

I'm excited to finally share my life completely with this woman.
