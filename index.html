My band. My family. These guys share the same love and passion for music that I do. The six of us have been performing together since April of 2013 and we don't plan on stopping any time soon. We released our first album titled Concrete World in May of 2014 and currently have a second album in the works. The album is entirely self produced (from the artwork to the mastering) and can be streamed on Soundcloud, Spotify, and Rdio or purchased on iTunes or CD Baby. My role in the band includes rhythm guitar and vocals, and I am also involved with the mixing and mastering of our recorded music.
